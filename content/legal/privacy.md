# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 14 May 2026_

OpenBook is operated by **OpenHouse AI Limited** (trading as **OpenBook**),
registered in Ireland. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what
you can do about it. Plain language; no fluff.

## What we collect

- **Account data** — when you sign in (Apple, Google or magic-link email)
  we store your email, your display name and the OAuth identifier from the
  provider.
- **Booking data** — for every booking you make: the business, the
  service, the time, the price and the booking status.
- **Wallet data** — store credits and packages issued to you by businesses
  you've used.
- **Device data** — basic information needed to make the app work (device
  type, language, push token if you opted in).
- **Diagnostic data** — error reports and aggregated usage so we can fix
  bugs. Never tied to your name or email.

We do **not** sell or rent your data, ever.

## How we use it

- To show your bookings, wallet balance and preferences across devices.
- To send transactional notifications (booking confirmed, slot opening,
  cancellation) — only the ones you've enabled.
- To process payments, via Stripe Ireland (we never see your card details).
- To improve the product (aggregate, anonymous analytics).

## Who we share it with

- The business you book with — they see your name, contact and booking
  details so they can serve you.
- Stripe — when you pay online, to process the charge.
- Supabase (our hosting and database provider) and Vercel (our app
  infrastructure), both bound by data-processing agreements.

We don't share your data with advertisers.

## Your rights

Under GDPR and Irish data protection law you can:

- Ask for a copy of the data we hold on you.
- Ask us to correct it.
- Ask us to delete it (use **Delete account** under Profile, or email us).
- Object to processing.

Email **sam@openhouseai.ie** to exercise any of these rights and we'll
respond within 30 days.

## Data retention

We keep booking records for as long as you have an account, and for up to
6 years after deletion if Irish accounting law requires it (Revenue
Commissioners). Diagnostic data is retained for 90 days.

## Cookies

The app uses essential cookies for sign-in only. No advertising trackers,
no third-party analytics in the app surface.
